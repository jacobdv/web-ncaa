The Four Factors

The Four Factors are statistics identified by Dean Oliver, a statistician and coach, as representing the keys to basketball success. Simply put, these four stats measure a team's ability to score points, hold onto the ball on offense, rebound on both ends of the floor, and get to the free throw line. While these statistics are positively regarded in general, using Oliver's weighting system to create a single statistic leads to a low correlation with win percentage.
